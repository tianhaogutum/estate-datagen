SCENARIO: Address in maintenance protocol does not match the building in the contract.

Generate a MaintenanceProtocol where the Anlagenstandort address is different
from the building address recorded in the associated MaintenanceContract.

RULES:
- The contract building address is:
  "Sachsenhäuser Landwehrweg 23, 60594 Frankfurt am Main"
- The protocol Anlagenstandort MUST use a clearly different address, e.g.:
  "Hanauer Landstraße 147, 60314 Frankfurt am Main"
  (different street, same city is sufficient — or even a different city)
- device_type must still match: "Klimaanlage (Split-System)"
- Ausfuehrende_Fachfirma should be the same service provider as the contract
- maintenance_date must fall within a plausible active contract period
- All other fields must be realistic and internally consistent
- The anomaly is intentional: the address mismatch indicates the technician
  serviced a different device/building than the one covered by this contract
